How to play

  • 1. Start the Game:Both players have a stack of facedown cards.
  • 2.Rounds:
  • •Player’s Turn:
  • 1.Player selects a stat from their top card.
    2.Announces the chosen stat.
  • •Computer’s Turn:
  • 1.Computer randomly selects a stat from its top card.
    2.Reveals the selected stat.
  • •Compare Stats:
  • 1.Reveal both top cards and compare stats.
    2.Higher value wins the round.
  • •Collect Cards:
  • 1.Winner takes both cards and places them at the bottom of their stack.
    2.Winner starts the next round by selecting a stat from their new top card.
  • •End of the Game:
  • Game ends when a player runs out of cards (they lose).
  • In case of a tie, cards go to a "tie pile." The next round's winner takes all the cards in the tie pile.
Enjoy!
